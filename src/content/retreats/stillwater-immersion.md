Stillwater is our flagship Bali edition — eight days among rice terraces and ancient
temple grounds in Ubud. The practice arc moves from awakening to surrender: vigorous
Ashtanga in the cool of the morning, restorative Yin as the light fades, and a daily
Nidra session that teaches the nervous system how to truly rest.

## Who it's for

Practitioners of any level who want depth over spectacle. Whether this is your first
retreat or your tenth, the small group size means the practice meets you where you are.

## What's included

Accommodation, all vegetarian meals, every practice session, and airport transfers
within the Ubud area. Flights are not included.
