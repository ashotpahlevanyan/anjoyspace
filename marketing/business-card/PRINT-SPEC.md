# Anjoy Space — NFC Business Card · Print Specification

A brief for producing our NFC business card. Please review and reply with a quote.

---

### 1. Product
Custom-printed **NFC card** in standard **CR80 credit-card size (85.6 × 54 mm)**, PVC, with an NFC chip embedded inside the card.

### 2. Artwork
Two **print-ready PDFs** are provided — one for the front, one for the back.
- Trim size **85.6 × 54 mm** with **3 mm bleed** all around (files are **91.6 × 60 mm** including bleed).
- Fonts are embedded; no additional prep required.

### 3. Printing
- **Double-sided**, **full-color (CMYK)**.
- **Matte finish / matte laminate.**

### 4. NFC chip
- **NTAG215** preferred (NTAG213 acceptable).
- Program the chip to open this URL: **https://anjoy.space/card**
  *(or supply the cards with the chip unprogrammed and we will program them ourselves).*

### 5. Design-critical requirements
- The background is a **dark, near-black full-bleed** color. It must print with **even coverage — no banding or roller marks**; matte laminate is required.
- The **QR code must stay dark modules on the light panel** — do **not** tint, recolor, or invert it, and do not shrink it below **~20 mm**. It must remain scannable.

### 6. What we need from you
- Price quote for a quantity of **[QUANTITY]** cards.
- Production turnaround time.
- A **proof** (physical or digital) for approval before the full run.
