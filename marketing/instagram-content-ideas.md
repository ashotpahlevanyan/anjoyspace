# Anjoy Space — Instagram & Content Ideas

> Living doc. We'll keep adding to it. Captured from the 2026-07-11 discussion.

---

## Guiding principle
**Authenticity over AI.** Anjoy is real presence, real teachers, real places. Use real
humans for anything a person *does* (practices, teaching, on-camera). Use AI/generative
work only for the *wrapper* — branded abstract visuals, editing, captions, scripts.

- ❌ AI-generated humans doing asanas — glitchy, can teach wrong alignment, reads as soulless.
- ✅ **Real short practices filmed with Ani** (and Yulia/Katya) — waits on Ani accepting the gift.

---

## Content pillars (rotate these)
1. **Real retreat footage** — sunrise practice, locations, ceremonies. #1 selling asset.
2. **Teacher Reels** — face-to-camera + short do-along practices ("60-sec reset with Ани",
   "breath before sleep", "morning spinal flow"). Builds the trust that converts to bookings.
3. **Free value** — a breath technique, a Nidra taste, "what to pack for a retreat".
   Repurpose the blog (`/blog`) into carousels/Reels.
4. **Transformation** — real participant testimonials (video ones already exist).
5. **The vision** — Armenia retreat centers (Lori + Gavar); reuse the animated SVG scenes
   from `/retreat-center`.

## Format priorities (2026)
- **Reels win reach** (pushed to non-followers) → post mostly Reels.
- **Carousels** for saves/depth (educational).
- **Stories** daily — connection, launches, countdowns, polls, Q&A.

## Growth levers
- Consistency (3–5×/week); strong 1-second hooks.
- Local + niche tags (#йогамосква #йогаретрит #yogaretreat), geotags.
- **Lead-magnet loop:** "comment ГИД → I'll DM the free Nidra guide" (guide lives at `/free`).
  Grows engagement + email list.
- Collabs: reshare teacher + past-guest content.
- Small **boosted Reel** → Moscow + yoga/wellness/travel interests (retreats = high LTV, ads can pay off).

## Measurement (already built)
- Link-in-bio with UTM → `anjoy.space/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio`.
- GA4 + Yandex Metrica show IG-driven traffic; first-touch UTM attribution tags any resulting lead.
