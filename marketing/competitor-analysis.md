# Anjoy Space — Competitor / Scope Analysis

> Adjacent SE-Asia yoga businesses studied for *scope* (features & revenue lines to learn from,
> not positioning to copy). Living doc. Last updated: 2026-07-18.

## Studied
- **The Yoga Barn** — Ubud, Bali · https://theyogabarn.com/
- **Vikasa** — Koh Samui, Thailand · https://vikasa.com/

Both are **physical destinations** (staff, rooms, café, spa). Anjoy is a curated retreat
*organizer* with a center still a vision (Lori/Gavar). So learn selectively — don't chase their full stack.

---

## Their scope

### The Yoga Barn
- **Offerings:** daily classes, private instruction, ecstatic dance; signature retreats + 3-day immersions;
  YTT 50/100/200hr; workshops/master classes/events (Quantum Flow, Handpan, Bali Spirit Festival);
  wellness/healing (Reiki, Thai massage, sound healing, tarot, spa); accommodation ("The Nest"); Garden Kafe.
- **Online:** YogaBarnONLINE — live sessions, 3-month subscription ("Seed to Blossom"), on-demand library (lifetime access).
- **Monetize:** drop-in passes, packages/memberships, retreats, YTT, workshops, accommodation, café,
  online subscription, gift cards, wellness à la carte.
- **Platform:** native mobile app (iOS/Android), online class registration, interactive schedule w/ filters,
  pricing pages, teacher profiles, blog, newsletter, testimonials, IG feed, TripAdvisor/WhatsApp.
- **Nav:** Classes · Retreats · Trainings · Workshops & Events · Wellness · Garden Kafe · Accommodation · Blog · About.

### Vikasa
- **Offerings:** YTT is the engine (200/300/100/50hr incl. Yin, Flow, Meditation, Sound, Reiki);
  wellness programs (Begin, Recharge, Vitox, Deepen); signature retreats (Evolve, Escape, Silent, Thrive,
  KOSTA, Karma Burning, Move) + "Vikations 365" custom dates; daily classes; Vita Spa; Life Café; cooking;
  excursions; Online Academy; mentorship.
- **Monetize:** all-inclusive retreat packages (room+meals+classes), tiered YTT, online academy subs,
  spa à la carte, room upcharges, early-bird discounts, **loyalty + advocate/referral**, **retreat-hosting commission**.
- **Platform:** online booking (reservations.vikasa.com) w/ real-time room availability, promo pricing tables,
  event calendar + countdowns, virtual room tours, blog, newsletter, live chat, testimonials/graduate hub,
  loyalty program, separate academy portal, multi-location (Samui/Phuket/Bangkok/Sri Lanka), persistent "BOOK NOW".
- **Nav:** Retreats · Trainings · Programs · Experience · Evolution · Locations · Contact.

## Prioritized "worth adding" (tuned to Anjoy's stage)

**🟢 Now (feasible, high ROI)**
1. **Online booking + deposit** — #1 platform gap; turns inquiry → committed (e.g., YooKassa deposit link).
2. **Loyalty / referral** — capture the **94% return rate**; "bring-a-friend" / returning-guest perk. Near-free money.
3. **Richer retreat detail** — what's **included/excluded**, accommodation, meals, **cancellation policy**.
4. **Real dates + "spots left" + countdown** on retreats (urgency; both use it).

**🟡 Strategic — design into the Lori/Gavar center**
5. **Retreat-hosting model** ⭐ — let *other* teachers run retreats at the center for a commission.
   Fills the calendar without owning all content; de-risks the center financially. The "Partner with us"
   seed already exists on `/retreat-center`. **Most valuable idea from both sites.**
6. Accommodation w/ room tiers + all-inclusive pricing (when built).
7. Spa / café / amenities as à-la-carte lines.

**🔵 Later (authority + recurring revenue, needs Ani onboard)**
8. **YTT** — biggest revenue + authority line for both; Ani-led, high-margin.
9. **Online academy** — a few recorded courses → recurring revenue + global reach.
10. **Programs-by-intention** framing (Reset / Deepen / Begin) — cheap positioning tweak.

**Cross-cutting:** list on **aggregators** (BookYogaRetreats, Retreat Guru, TripAdvisor) — discovery for the
"yoga retreat abroad" searcher Anjoy can't reach organically yet.

---

## Honest framing
Yoga Barn & Vikasa are destinations with staff/rooms/cafés; Anjoy is a curated organizer with a center on the
horizon. Near-term wins = **booking+deposit, loyalty/referral, richer retreat detail**. The strategic one =
**bake the retreat-hosting model into the Lori/Gavar plan.** Everything else (YTT, academy) waits on Ani + the center.
